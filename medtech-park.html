    MedTech Park is Singapore's first hub dedicated to the medical technology (medtech) industry, serving to cluster medtech manufacturers, product owners and service providers together for synergies and collaborations. Strategically located in close proximity to major residential estates and mass transportation services, the 7.4-hectare Park also supports the industry's employment base. ​​

    Testbedding footprint: MedTech Park

    This is a cloud-based system that allows JTC to centrally and remotely monitor, analyse, and optimise estate and building systems on the facilities management front.

    The system leverages data, analytics and sensors across our buildings and estates to provide insight into how the systems are operating, thus allowing JTC to make decisions to ensure optimal operations of the buildings’ systems and essential and critical services. This in turn reduces energy usage, improves the comfort of JTC’s tenants and supports JTC’s productivity and efficiency in managing facilities.

    Testbedding footprint: MedTech Park / MedTech Hub

    Geogrids are geosynthetic materials used to reinforce soils and similar materials. They can be used for reinforcing soil in civil engineering projects and are highly resistant to biological and chemical degradation.

    With potential to reduce road construction time by 36%, JTC is looking at utilising geogrids to increase the construction speed of its industrial infrastructural projects.

    Testbedding footprint: MedTech Park / MedTech Hub
