    • Drone-based 3D Mapping
      for Change Detection

    This proof-of-concept investigates the use of drones in JTC’s estates and developments to monitor environmental issues like ponding and illicit activities, including dumping and illegal extension of land. The test-bed will explore the use of photogrammetry; using photography for surveying and mapping to ascertain measurements between objects.

    Testbedding footprint: Jurong Port Road
