    • Industrial Pre-fabrication
      /Precast Concrete System

    The JTC Surface Engineering Hub (SEH) is a first-of-its-kind, multi-tenanted development that integrates the entire value chain of companies in the surface engineering industry. Its strategy of clustering companies within the same industry allows the SEH to optimise land use, achieve collaborative synergies, as well as facilitate the sharing of know-how amongst surface engineering companies, suppliers, solution providers, and other complementary businesses. In addition, its centralised wastewater treatment plant (CWTP) allows companies to reduce space requirements, operational costs and upfront capital investments.​

    Testbedding footprint: Surface Engineering Hub

    In tandem with national efforts to increase construction and building productivity, SEH is the first among JTC’s developments that makes use of extensive pre-cast, mechanisation and prefabrication techniques to improve construction efficiency. The standardised precast structural elements are columns, beams and slabs with other items such as emergency staircase walls, partition walls, etc. Prefabricated elements include exhaust fume ducts, M&E Service trays, etc.

    Adopting the System, construction time is reduced by 3 months with manpower savings of about 23% (135 workers as compared to 175 workers). The corresponding cost saving based on manpower alone is about 23%.
