CleanTech Park

The RDI Group is developing the investment part of its business rapidly and conducts business in many industries in Russia and many countries around the world, including Austria and Germany.

The main directions of investment activity in the RDI Group are development and investments in digital technologies of the future. The RDI Group also actively participates in and develops such promising areas as investment in real estate and oil production, provision of consulting services, creative agency services, and fitness.

The RDI Group unites several companies that operate in different segments allowing for risk-resistant business. The group companies conduct their business in the most promising sectors of the economy. A unique asset portfolio allows the group to work in the scope of the complex development of large territories. The total assets of the companies are worth more than 115 billion rubles.

The activity of each of the subsidiaries is an integral part of the business to increase the value of its assets and maximize profits across the Group.

TRIAD Berlin

Creative Agency

The RDI Group is one of shareholders of the TRIAD creative agency that has offices in Berlin and Shanghai. The agency currently employs more than 180 staff members.

TRIAD has been designing and producing exhibitions, museums, theme parks, trade fairs, retail spaces and events across the globe since 1994. The activities of TRIAD cover the entire range of project development services – from strategic consulting, scenographic development, concept design, and media production to construction planning and engineering.
