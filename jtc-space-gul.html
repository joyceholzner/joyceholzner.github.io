    JTC Space @ Gul

    JTC Space @ Gul offers factory units with unique structural provisions that allow production activities traditionally carried out on land to be vertically integrated over multiple floors, occupying a much smaller land footprint and optimising land use. The three-storey development comes with high building specifications that provide maximum flexibility for industrialists to carry out a wide range of manufacturing activities.

    JTC Space @ Gul

    Development of a mobile robot system (A-Conquars). Test-bedding involves using a mobile robot to conduct construction quality inspection. JTC targets to achieve a 15% reduction in the time taken to identify and rectify defects and manpower by 50%. The field trial will be conducted at JTC Space @ Gul.

    JTC Space @ Gul

    Development of a mobile robot system equipped with a novel long reach mechanism for high-ceiling and wall painting applications. JTC aims to achieve a reduction in painting time by 25% and manpower by 50%. The robot will be test-bedded in a suitable construction site at the end of project. The test-bed location will be at CleanTech Two while the field trial will be at JTC Space @ Gul.
