    • Solar Licensing Test-bed

    Strategically located in the heart of Jurong with excellent connectivity to other parts of Singapore, as well as a wide range of amenities nearby, iHUB is an ideal site for companies seeking the space to breathe and generate new ideas. In addition, the development provides an exclusive environment designed to support business park uses, such as research & development, software development and industrial training. Given the conservation status by URA in 2005, it remains a key landmark today that symbolises Singapore's economic and industrial progress.

    This test-bed explores the direct connection of photovoltaic generators to grid networks operated by Singapore Power Grid (SPPG). This will enable decoupling of roof top space from solar energy consumption, hence encouraging businesses to purchase solar energy even if they do not have roof space. It also allows owners of roof spaces to generate an additional revenue stream if they do not require solar power.
